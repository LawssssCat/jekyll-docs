Step 1

Prepare article object list.

Each article obj needs to have the following attributes.

keyrequirevalue
article.titletrue 
article.urltrue 
article.covertrue 

e.g.

the code of article obj declare

the attributes of article obj

  "title": "Page - Single",
  "cover": "/assets/images/axure/page-single.jpg",
  "url": "/page/single.html"
